# 🛡️ GigShield AI

### Protecting the People Who Keep India Moving

---

## 📍 The Problem

India’s delivery partners working with platforms like Zomato, Swiggy, and Blinkit are the backbone of the on-demand economy. Their earnings depend entirely on how many hours they can stay active on the road.

But their income is fragile.

A sudden heatwave.
Unexpected heavy rainfall.
Severe air pollution.

These aren’t just inconveniences — they directly reduce working hours and cut down income by 20–30%.

And today, there is **no structured protection** for this type of loss.

---

## 💡 Our Approach

GigShield AI is an **AI-powered parametric insurance platform** designed specifically for gig workers.

Instead of traditional insurance (which is slow and claim-heavy), this system works on:

* Automated trigger-based payouts
* AI-driven risk assessment
* Weekly, affordable pricing
* Zero manual claims

The goal is simple:
**Protect income when external conditions make work impossible.**

---

## 👤 Target Persona

**Food Delivery Partner (Zomato / Swiggy ecosystem)**

### Typical Work Pattern:

* 8–10 working hours daily
* Earnings depend on completed deliveries
* Works outdoors in unpredictable conditions

### Key Challenges:

* No earnings if unable to work

## 💰 Weekly Premium Model

The pricing model is designed around a **weekly cycle**, matching how gig workers earn.

### Formula:

```bash
Premium = 30 + (Risk Score × 50)
```

### Why Weekly?

* Matches real earning cycles
* Easy to adopt
* Low commitment
* Scalable pricing

---

## ⚡ Parametric Trigger System

This platform uses **event-based triggers instead of manual claims**.

| Trigger            | Condition             | Outcome          |
| ------------------ | --------------------- | ---------------- |
| High Temperature   | Above 35°C            | ₹150 payout      |
| Heavy Rain         | Triggered event / API | ₹200 payout      |
| Pollution (future) | AQI threshold         | Automatic payout |

No paperwork. No waiting. No disputes.
